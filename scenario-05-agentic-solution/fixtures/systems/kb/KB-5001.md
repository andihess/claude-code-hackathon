# KB-5001 — Suspected phishing or account compromise

**Category:** security

## When this applies
Any report of a suspicious email, credential theft, unexpected account activity,
malware, or a device/account believed to be compromised.

## Critical rule
**Never auto-resolve and never sit in a category queue.** Security signals always
**escalate** to `security-incident`, regardless of stated urgency or confidence.

## Resolution
1. Do not click links or forward the suspicious message further.
2. Preserve evidence (headers, timestamps, screenshots).
3. Escalate immediately to the security-incident team for containment.
4. If credentials may be exposed, flag the account for a forced reset by that team.

## Severity guidance
**P1** if a compromise is active or in progress (credentials confirmed stolen,
unauthorized access observed). Otherwise **P2** pending investigation. Escalate either way.
