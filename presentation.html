An agent that reads an inbound IT ticket, verifies who sent it, checks the knowledge base, and decides — auto-resolve, route, or escalate — before a human ever sees it.
The problem we picked: IT helpdesk requests arrive by ticket, chat, and "urgent" email to the CIO, and get hand-triaged one at a time. Time-to-first-response is measured in hours nobody is proud of.
